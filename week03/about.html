Thrills and Safety Since 1995

Our mission is simple: to provide an unforgettable, high-adrenaline, yet secure, adventure. We believe in respect for the river, rigorous safety protocols, and creating lifelong memories for our guests. We are guides, river rats, and safety experts first.

Our experienced team of guides are all certified in advanced wilderness first aid and river rescue techniques, ensuring that every trip down the river is as safe as it is exciting.

History

Founded in 1995 by two local river guides, RiverRun Adventures started with two rafts and a dream. Our passion for the wild waterways quickly grew a loyal following. Over the decades, we've expanded our fleet and our team, but our core commitment to thrilling, ethical, and sustainable rafting remains the same.
